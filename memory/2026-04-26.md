### console_charts_guide.md (345 lines)
Comprehensive guide explaining all 4 dashboard charts:

1. **Equity Chart**: Daily total equity progression ($100k → $105k, +5%)
2. **Drawdown % Chart**: Peak-to-current drawdown (ideal: 0-5%, warning: 5-10%, danger: 10%+)
3. **Open Positions Chart**: Number of open positions (ideal: 5-15 symbols)
4. **Signals/Orders Chart**: Conversion rate tracking (ideal: 20-30%)

**Key Insight on Drawdown**:
- Drawdown is a **risk management indicator** (portfolio-level loss from peak)
- NOT a market movement detector (use macro_regime feature instead)
- NOT an individual stock deterioration detector (use unrealized_pnl_pct instead)
- Shows: risk tolerance, recovery ability, strategy stability
- Interpretation: 0-5% normal, 5-10% caution, 10%+ danger
