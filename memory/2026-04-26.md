**Key Insight on Drawdown**:
- Drawdown is a **risk management indicator** (portfolio-level loss from peak)
- NOT a market movement detector (use macro_regime feature instead)
- NOT an individual stock deterioration detector (use unrealized_pnl_pct instead)
- Shows: risk tolerance, recovery ability, strategy stability
- Interpretation: 0-5% normal, 5-10% caution, 10%+ danger
